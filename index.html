inuit

"inuit" is a side-scrolling platforming puzzle game with equal parts problem solving and platforming skills. This version features 12 levels of rapidly increasing difficulty. Join Nap the lonely inuit on his adventure to kill the seals. You must take advantage of environmental factors to finish the job. All of this while racing against the clock for the best time.
